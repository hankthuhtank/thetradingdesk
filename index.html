Candlestick Decoder
Context is everything. Patterns are just clues.
Bullish Engulfing
Strong reversal at support
Large bullish candle completely swallows the prior bearish candle. Buyers took full control. Highest probability at major support after a downtrend.
Bearish Engulfing
Strong reversal at resistance
Large bearish candle engulfs the previous bullish one. Sellers seized control. Most powerful near resistance after an uptrend.
Hammer
Potential bottom reversal
Small body near the high with long lower wick. Shows strong rejection of lower prices. Best after a clear downtrend at support. Needs confirmation.
Shooting Star
Potential top reversal
Small body near the low with long upper wick. Buyers pushed high but sellers rejected it hard. Watch for confirmation at resistance.
Doji
Indecision / equilibrium
Open ≈ Close. Represents balance between buyers and sellers. Most powerful at support/resistance or after extended trends. Context is everything.
Morning Star
Strong 3-candle bullish reversal
Bearish candle → small indecisive candle → large bullish candle. One of the more reliable reversal patterns when it appears at support after a downtrend.
Remember: No pattern works in isolation. Always combine with market structure, volume, and your rules.
