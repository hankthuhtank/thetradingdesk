Works for shares or contracts: for options/futures, treat "entry/stop" as the contract price and remember each contract controls a multiplier (options ×100). Risk amount = account × risk%. Size = risk ÷ per-unit stop distance.

TOOL // EXPECTANCY LAB

Expectancy Lab

The math doesn't care about your feelings. Find your breakeven and your edge.

Breakeven win rate
—
Expectancy / trade
—

Breakeven win rate = 1 ÷ (1 + R). Expectancy = (win% × reward) ? (loss% × risk). A positive expectancy repeated with discipline is the entire game — everything else is decoration.
