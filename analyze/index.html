Methodology — nothing is a black box

GEX per strike

GEX$ = Γ × OI × 100 × S² × 0.01 × dealer_sign — dollar gamma notional per 1% spot move. Γ comes straight from CBOE delayed quotes when present (exchange-computed), otherwise Black-Scholes (r=4.5%, q=1.2%, mid-IV). dealer_sign = 1 − 2×(fraction of OI assumed short); the default Standard preset gives calls +1, puts −1 (Pika / Barney).

Full gamma profile, zero-γ flip, walls

Instead of only bucketing current-spot gamma (the classic free-tool flaw), Kairos re-prices Black-Scholes Γ for every contract across a 61-point spot grid (±15%) each cycle. The zero-gamma flip is the interpolated sign change of net dealer gamma nearest spot; call wall / put wall are the largest positive / negative strike nodes. Air pockets are contiguous strike runs with |GEX| < 5% of the King.

VEX (vanna) approximation

vanna = −e^(−qT) φ(d1) d2 / σ, then VEX$ = vanna × OI × 100 × S × 0.01 × dealer_sign — dollar delta impulse per 1 vol-point. It is an approximation (no vol-surface dynamics, no sticky-strike/delta modelling) and is labelled as such. Alignment states: +G/+V pin & drift, +G/−V pin with vol-pop risk, −G/−V full accelerant (crash fuel), −G/+V squeeze fuel.

Velocity & node lifecycle

Kairos snapshots per-strike GEX locally every refresh. The badge on a row is the % change of that node over your selected window (1–60m). Nodes brighten/thicken as they build, fade as they decay. History lives in your browser only — it starts accumulating the moment you open the app, so early-session velocity is "warming up". Fresh vs tested: a level touched by price since seeding is marked tested in deep dive.

Node taxonomy

King = largest |GEX| node in window. Gatekeepers = nodes ≥35% of King strictly between spot and King. Pika = positive (teal/gold), Barney = negative (purple). Air pockets = near-zero zones price can traverse fast.

Idea engine (Surfaced)

Deterministic and printed in full on every card (tap a card to expand reasoning): regime from net GEX sign & spot-vs-flip; direction toward King in +γ (magnet), with momentum through air pockets in −γ; contract = nearest |Δ|≈0.45, OI≥100, spread≤12% of mid; confidence 0–100 = 50 + node dominance (≤15) + G/V alignment (≤10) + velocity confirmation (≤10) + liquidity (≤5) + room-to-target (≤10), penalties for wide markets and mid-window earnings-style binaries are out of scope (no free earnings feed — flagged as unknown). R:R = distance to target node ÷ distance to invalidation (flip or opposing gatekeeper). These are data-derived scenario notes, not directives.

Data honesty

CBOE delayed quotes: OI updates once daily (pre-market), greeks/IV/prices ~15-min delayed, free, no key. Finnhub free tier: live trade prices for stocks/ETFs (SPX proxied as SPY×10, VIX stays CBOE-delayed). The gamma profile is re-priced with live IV mids every cycle even though OI is static intraday — that is exactly what the paid tools do too. No free intraday volume feed exists, so VWAP is replaced by session TWAP and volume profile is omitted rather than faked.
